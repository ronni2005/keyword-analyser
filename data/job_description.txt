JOB TITLE: Senior Data Analyst
COMPANY: NovaFintech Inc.
LOCATION: Remote / San Francisco, CA
TYPE: Full-time

ABOUT NOVAFINTECH
NovaFintech is a leading financial technology company dedicated to democratizing access to investment tools. We leverage big data and machine learning to provide personalized financial advice to millions of users.

ROLE OVERVIEW
We are seeking a skilled Senior Data Analyst to join our Growth Team. In this role, you will be responsible for analyzing user behavior, building predictive models, and creating dashboards that drive strategic decision-making. The ideal candidate has a strong foundation in statistics, proficiency in Python/SQL, and a passion for storytelling with data.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and maintain scalable data pipelines to support real-time analytics.
- Perform deep-dive analysis on user acquisition and retention metrics.
- Develop and deploy machine learning models to predict customer lifetime value (CLV).
- Must be good at deep learning and natural language processing
- Create interactive dashboards and reports for stakeholders using Tableau or Power BI.
- Collaborate with engineering and product teams to define data requirements and A/B test strategies.

QUALIFICATIONS
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Statistics, Computer Science, Economics, or a related field.
- 5+ years of experience in data analysis or a related role.
- Expert-level proficiency in SQL and Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn).
- Must be good at deep learning and natural language processing
- Strong experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker).
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and big data technologies (Spark, Snowflake).
- Excellent communication skills with the ability to translate technical findings for non-technical audiences.

BENEFITS
- Competitive salary and equity package.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- 401(k) matching.
- Flexible work hours and remote-friendly culture.
- Professional development stipend.

HOW TO APPLY
Please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing a recent data project you are proud of to careers@novafintech.com.
